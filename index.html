About the legend

Sushant Singh Rajput (1986-forever) was an Indian actor, dancer, entrepreneur, and philanthropist.
Sushant, a champion of science, space, and technology, aimed to address the socio-economic needs in diverse segments through various initiates & worked on the convergence of Intellectual Property and Emerging Technologies.

The actor envisions a connected world through technological innovations and extends its benefits to the grass-root level. A Gold medalist at the Physics Olympiad, Sushant Singh Rajput is passionate about a wide range of scientific disciplines such as polymath, quantum physics, trigonometry, aeronautics, astrophysics, etc.
Sushant has recently completed training at NASA (National Aeronautics and Space Administration) and has initiated a campaign for education sUSHAnt4education.
